Facial Paralysis Detection


Facial paralysis detection is beneficial because it detects asymmetry in symptoms in face. First of all the system takes photos of face, which is then processed and detect facial paralysis using our trained model. And then it will show result, if any disease is their then it will notify us. The facial paralysis is detected using the Inception V3 model (Convolutional Neural Network). In terms of testing and validation accuracy,the intermediate results are presented. It is observed that the best training accuracy for Inception, 99.33 percent is obtained at the 31st epoch and the best test accuracy, 89.87 percent is obtained at the 25th epoch and for cnn best training accuracy, 98.57 percent is obtained at the 40th epoch and the best test accuracy, 89.87 percent is obtained at the 22nh epoch. Lastly, we conclude our result.
